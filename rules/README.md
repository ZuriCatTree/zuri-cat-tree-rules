# Rules

1. You must be 18 or over to join. To join, please refer to the [Joining flow](../joining-flow/README.md).
2. Discrimination in any shape or form is not tolerated. Please, do not use racial slurs in the chat. Admins know how to differ between jokes and discrimination, but may take action in their own discretion.
3. Do not incite violence.
4. Do not create drama, provoke other members, or make a scene. If another individual is causing you harm, please contact an admin. Please refer to the [admins list](../staff/admins.md).
5. It is extremely forbidden to dox others.
6. Do not speak as a channel, but rather as your actual account.
7. Do not ask for special privileges. If you want to apply to staff, please refer to the [admin list](../staff/admins.md) to contact the owner or an admin.
8. It is forbidden to join and leave repeatedly. After 3 iterations, you will be banned.
9. Do not advertise without permission from an *Admin* or *Mod*. Getting permission once does not mean you can keep advertising indefinitely. Every ad must be approved beforehand.
10. Do not spam the chat. Albums are allowed for up to 10 images. There is no set time limit for when you can send more, but try not to spam the chat. Allow for activity or send them in DMs. Never speaking in the chat and only sending images ONLY is considered spam.
11. Real-life NSFW content is allowed when it is spoilered. This includes images, videos and gifs, as well anything that may be considered suggestive or lewd. Please consult an Admin if you are unsure.
12. Political Discussions are allowed with a couple exceptions, as long as the wording in the discussion is respectful and members exercise caution when discussion anything that may be considered uncomfortable. **Admins can shut down a discussion at their discretion.**
13. Please refrain from posting any images or art containing [Banned Kinks](./careful-with-these.md#kinks) or [Banned Artists](./careful-with-these.md#banned-artists).
14. Sharing Paysite Content is forbidden unless publicly posted somewhere. You have permission to share art like that with the artist's permission.
15. AI "Art" is forbidden.
16. Individuals with enough complaints against their persona may find themselves kicked, timed out or banned from the chats and network.
